About Us

A Landmark Residential Opportunity

Orion Galaxy is India’s first project to offer fully customizable apartments. Strategically located off the Golf Course Extension Road at Sector 68, Sohna Road, Gurgaon, it offers unprecedented connectivity. With the upcoming peripheral Expressway and a planned metro station within walking distance, an apartment here is simply the best value in the neighbourhood.

From Vision to Reality: The PSY Transformation

With a vision to breathe new life into incomplete assets, PSY took over Orion Galaxy and transformed it into a thriving space. The project now stands as a symbol of successful turnaround and timely delivery, featuring modern commercial spaces, retail outlets, and premium amenities designed for today’s entrepreneurs and professionals along the fast-developing Sohna Road corridor.
